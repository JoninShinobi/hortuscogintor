Market Positioning Conclusion

HC-MA-01F

Unique Market Position Identified

Hortus Cognitor occupies a distinctive market space with no direct competitors.

Partial Competitive Overlap:

  • The Apricot Centre: Shared agricultural content, different transformation approach
  • Change in Nature: Shared transformation focus, different delivery methodology

Unique Competitive Advantages:

  • Price Accessibility: £200 total (£40/session) vs £335-£864+ competitors - serves different market segment focused on personal transformation rather than professional development
  • Geographic Accessibility: Local Leicester delivery vs distant/online options
  • Instructor Authority: Psychology + council + farming background
  • Integrated Approach: Personal transformation + practical agricultural skills
  • Community Format: 5 monthly sessions vs intensive residential programs or weekly commitments
